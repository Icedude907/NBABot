NBA Commands


nba scores

Shows you the live scores for today.

nba boxscore [team]

Shows you basic stats on that team in the game today. E.g. nba boxscore PHX

nba player-stats [player]

Shows you stats on that player. E.g. nba player-stats LeBron James

nba player-info [player]

Shows you basic information on that player like height, weight and draft pick. E.g. nba player-info LeBron James

nba standings

Shows you the current league standings.

nba standings west

Shows you the current west standings.

nba standings east

Shows you the current east standings.

nba roster [team]

Shows you the current roster for that team. E.g. nba roster PHX

Other Commands


nba help

Gives you the list of commands and the link to this page.

nba ping

Just gives the ping which is how long it takes for the bot to respond.

nba uptime

Gives you how long the bot has been online (in its current session).

nba invite

Gives you the link to add NBABot to your servers.

nba vote

Gives you the link to vote for NBABot.

nba github

Gives you the link to the GitHub of NBABot. Feel free to contribute.
