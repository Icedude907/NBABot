NBA Commands

nba scores

Shows you the live scores for today.

nba boxscore [team]

Shows you basic stats on that team in the game today. E.g. nba boxscore PHX

nba player-stats [player]

Shows you stats on that player. E.g. nba player-stats LeBron James

nba player-info [player]

Shows you basic information on that player like height, weight and draft pick. E.g. nba player-info LeBron James
